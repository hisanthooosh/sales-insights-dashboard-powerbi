📊 Power BI Sales Dashboard

May 2025 – Present

🔍 Project Overview

This dashboard helps visualize sales, profit, and product performance across regions. It provides interactive visuals like top products, monthly sales trends, and region-wise insights using DAX and Power BI.

  • Power BI
  • Excel
  • DAX

✨ Key Features

  • Interactive KPI Cards (Sales, Profit, Units Sold)
  • Monthly Sales Trends
  • Top 10 Products Chart
  • Region-wise Sales Map
  • Dynamic Filters and Slicers
